Local Belief

Faith, Legend, and Ritual

The hermitage at the summit is dedicated to Saint John the Baptist. According to local tradition, visitors ring the bell three times upon arrival to ward off evil and bring good fortune. Legends surrounding footprints, miracles, and protection at sea continue to shape the spiritual identity of the island.

Game of Thrones

From Sacred Island to Global Screen

In the TV series Game of Thrones, Gaztelugatxe was featured as Dragonstone, the ancestral home of House Targaryen. Although digital effects enhanced the setting, the dramatic cliffs and bridge brought international attention, boosting tourism and redefining the island's global image.
